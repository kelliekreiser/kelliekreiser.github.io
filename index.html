About Me

Hi, I'm Kellie!

I'm an AI enthusiast with a passion for developing creative applications powered by artificial intelligence. My journey began with a curiosity about what superpowers AI could provide.

Currently, I'm most interested in exploring how working artists use AI tools in their creative work. I love speaking with people who are pushing the boundaries of what the layperson can do with artificial intelligence. Does this describe you? Let's talk!

When I'm not tinkering, you can find me at the pottery wheel or trying to keep the cats from wreaking havoc.

Feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate on a project or just chat about AI!
